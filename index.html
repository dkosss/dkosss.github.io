Predicting a Win in League of Legends

League of Legends is a massive MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena). Esentially a 5v5 roleplaying game where you can play as Champions of the arena in select roles. The point of the game is to attack and destroy the enemy Nexus. The first team to dstroy the other teams Nexus wins the game. However, it can take time to get to the enemy Nexus. Having to push through enemy turrets, and inhibitors. There are also "neutral" objectives throughout the map which give your team buffs if killed. Our goal is to take the given data provided from Leagues API, and predict a win given fields such as first blood, first inhibitor, first tower, and so forth. The data from my dataset can be found here: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/datasnaek/league-of-legends?select=games.csv
